Projects

The "Escape The Grey" Puzzle Box

Routine corporate environments often lack creativity and inspiration, leading to feelings of disempowerment. "Escape the Grey" is a single-player experience where the player must complete a series of interactive tasks to escape a dull corporate office and reach the freedom of nature.


Tools:
Unity, C#, GitHub, Blender, Maya

The "Happy and Flappy" game

Imagine being a bird finally breaking free from its cage. Your goal is to flap your wings and navigate through a series of challenging obstacles, earning points based on how many coins you collect. The farther you go, the higher your score, testing both your skill and determination in a thrilling flight to freedom while also getting your arm workout.


Tools:
Unity, C#, GitHub, Maya

Airship relaxing space

This project was created as a place for relaxation — what could be better than a quiet summer evening with the gentle sound of crickets and a soft breeze? It offers a calming, immersive environment designed to help the player slow down, unwind, and enjoy a peaceful moment away from everyday stress.


Tools:
Unity, Substance Painter, C#, GitHub, Maya, Procreate

Future Property in VR

Future Property in VR is an interactive property showcase that lets clients explore future real estate projects in a fully immersive environment. It transforms presentations into engaging experiences, helping clients better understand design, scale, and atmosphere.


Tools:
Unreal, C#, GitHub, Maya, Substance Painter

Sleeping beauty in AR

Concept: Reinterpret museum objects as part of a “cabinet of curiosities” where each artifact tells a unique story enhanced by dance and movement. Participants experience a digital exhibition where objects come to life through choreographed motion, interactive installations, and spatial storytelling that invite them to explore the narrative through movement.


Tools:
Unity, C#, GitHub, Blender

Shanti

The Yoga Resort space in VR was designed as an alternative form of relaxation, offering a mindful break from everyday work. Here, users can ground themselves and find calmness while surrounded by a soothing, contemplative environment. They can listen to the crackling of burning candles, join a yoga session, or immerse themselves in meditation accompanied by the tranquil sounds of a singing bowl.


Tools:
Unity, C#, GitHub, Maya

Spells Wizard VR

Imagine yourself as a wizard, tasked with defending the world against sinister creatures trying to break into our realm. Harness the power of spellcasting through drawing, unleash a variety of magical effects, and stand as the last line of defense to save the world from its enemies.


Tools:
Unity, C#, GitHub, Maya

Witch's House

3D modelling project. This 3D witch’s house was inspired by the Halloween season and the warm, mysterious atmosphere of autumn. Every detail—from the hand-crafted materials to the glowing candles and interior lighting—was created entirely in Maya, with a focus on capturing both mood and storytelling.


Tools:
Maya
